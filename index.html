My dream job would be to work in the electric automobile/aerospace/solar industry. I want to work at a company that values pushing the technological envelope and is disrupting the industry’s norm. The company should value its employees, encourage self-improvement while providing room for expansion from within. Knowing that a company is proud of its employees and will do anything to keep them happy and working hard is a huge plus to personal mental health and stability.

As a graphic designer with 8+ years of experience, I am familiar with the field I am moving into and will bring what I know from the design side to the technical side of web development. I am committed to constantly looking for new ways to improve myself and how I go about my work.

When I am not working on freelance design projects, I enjoy woodworking or other creative projects to keep myself busy. I am also constantly researching different aerospace and technology companies that are changing the norm and making the world a better place to live.
